Juventus Fan Clubs

United by a great passion

Juventus Official Fan Clubs (JOFC) are the only Fan Clubs officially recognized by Juventus,
representing a true point of reference and territorial aggregation for fans around the world who share the same passion for Juventus colors.


More Info

Services

Thanks to affiliation, the Official Fan Clubs can offer many advantages to all their members: ticketing services for home and away matches,
Juventus Card request at a discounted price, exclusive events and activities attendance, and much more.

Want to join us?

Become a JOFC member

To become a JOFC member it is necessary to contact one of the Official Fan Clubs and register in. Once subscribed, each member can refer to the same Official Fan Club to request any reserved services throughout the year.
The registration fee is established by each Official Fan Club and the affiliation is valid for the entire sports season. Members are not allowed to switch to another Official Fan Club during the same season.
